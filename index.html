Find mental health support nearby. Or try something right where you are.

Roam is a mental health resource finder for San Francisco and the East Bay. It helps you discover free and low-cost places to go — and when going somewhere isn't realistic, it offers coping skills you can try right now, personalized to how you're feeling today.

Two ways to feel better today.

A quick mood check-in at the start of your day shapes both sides — so what you see matches where you actually are, not a generic list.

Find a place to go

Browse free and low-cost yoga, meditation, peer support, counseling, community events, and more across SF and the East Bay — filtered by distance, cost, and how you like to show up.

Try something right now

When going somewhere isn't an option, the Coping tab has breathing exercises, grounding techniques, gentle movement, and more. Pick a few for today and check them off as you go.

Personalized to your mood

A short check-in — "What sounds good right now?" — adjusts what the app surfaces, whether that's a quiet meditation class nearby or a 3-minute breathing exercise you can do at your desk.

Crisis support, always there

988, the Crisis Text Line, and the SF Warmline are one tap away from anywhere in the app — free, confidential, available now.
