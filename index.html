Built around how you actually feel.

A mood check-in at the start of your day shapes what you see — so the suggestions match your energy, not a generic list.

Resources near you

Browse free yoga, meditation, peer support, counseling, and more — filtered by distance, cost, and how you like to access things.

Coping skills

A small library of self-guided techniques — breathing, grounding, gentle movement — you can pick a few to try throughout the day.

Map view

See everything plotted on a map so you know exactly what's walkable from where you are right now.

Saved places

Bookmark programs and spaces you want to come back to, without having to remember where you found them.

Your preferences

Tell Roam your area, your budget, and how you like to show up — solo, around others, drop-in or scheduled — and it adjusts what it shows you.

Crisis support

988, the Crisis Text Line, and the SF Warmline are always one tap away — free, confidential, available now.
