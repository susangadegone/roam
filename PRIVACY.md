# Roam Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 27, 2026

Roam ("the App") helps people find free and low-cost mental health resources in San Francisco and the East Bay, and offers tools like mood check-ins and coping skills to support small daily steps. Your privacy matters to us, so here's exactly what data the App handles.

## 1. Information We Collect

**Account screen:** Roam shows a sign-up/sign-in screen that asks for an email address and password to create a sense of an account. As currently implemented, these values are used only to validate the form (e.g., checking email format and password length) and are not saved or transmitted anywhere — not to a server, not even to local storage. Only a flag indicating that you've completed this screen is stored on your device.

**Information you provide:** Mood check-ins, saved resources, saved coping skills, and activity/streak history you create while using the App. All of this is stored locally on your device.

**Location information:** With your permission, Roam uses your device's location to show nearby mental health resources on the map and to sort/filter results by distance. You can deny or revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings; the App will still function, but distance-based features will be limited.

**Information we do not collect:** We do not collect advertising identifiers, analytics/tracking data, or browsing behavior. We do not sell or share your data with third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

## 2. How Your Data Is Stored

All of your personal data — saved resources, mood check-ins, coping-skill history, points/streaks, and account screen state — is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not transmitted to or stored on any Roam server.

The App does make outbound network requests to fetch the catalog of mental health resources (names, locations, descriptions) from our content database. These requests retrieve public resource listings only — they do not include or transmit any of your personal information, mood check-ins, saved items, or account details.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above only to:
- Show you relevant, nearby mental health resources.
- Let you save resources and coping skills for quick access.
- Track your own mood check-ins and activity streaks to support your personal progress.
- Personalize onboarding and in-app guidance.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the features above.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. Because your personal data stays on your device, we have no access to it and cannot share what we don't have.

## 5. Your Choices and Controls

- **Location:** Enable or disable at any time via iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services.
- **Deleting your data:** Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data (saved resources, check-ins, streaks, account screen state).
- **Account:** No account credentials are stored anywhere, on your device or otherwise, so there is nothing to delete on our end.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Roam is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. Data Security

Because your information is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's own security features (e.g., passcode, Face ID/Touch ID, device encryption). We recommend keeping your device's OS updated and secured.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves (for example, if we add cloud sync or real account features in the future). We'll update the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, notify users in-app.
